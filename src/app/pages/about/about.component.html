Company Overview

Nellai Fresh Milk, founded in 2025 in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu, is dedicated to delivering farm-fresh dairy products directly to consumers. Our goal is to eliminate middlemen, support local farmers, and ensure every home receives healthy, natural, and chemical-free milk.

Our Vision

At Nellai Fresh Milk, our vision is to create a strong connection between local farmers and families, encouraging sustainable farming practices and making pure milk available at an affordable price ‚Äì with absolutely no preservatives.
