Our Process

We collect fresh cow’s milk in the evening and immediately fill it into clean and sterilized glass bottles. The milk is 100% pure with no water added or preservatives. Before filling, all bottles are thoroughly cleaned and prepared to ensure hygiene. Finally, the milk is delivered straight to your doorstep, fresh and safe.
